## ✅ How the Website Works

#### The platform supports two primary workflows:

1. Users working as sale-staff inside a company

2. Users who own a company and manage multiple shops

### A. Sale-Staff Workflow

After logging in, the user selects a shop (or group of shops) they have permission to access.
The company owner is responsible for inviting users to their organization.

Access Permissions (Sale-Staff role)

#### a. Message

Reply and advise customers

Interact in chat

Convert conversation into closing orders

#### b. Orders

View all orders belonging to the shops they are assigned to

Create or edit orders

Cannot delete orders (to prevent intentional data loss)

Can update order status (including cancelation)

#### c. Personal Profile

View and modify personal information

Select the company they work for

View performance history: salary, bonuses, diligence, penalties

#### d. Products

View available inventory

Inspect product attributes: color, size, selling price, etc.

#### e. Settings

Change password

### B. Manager Workflow

In addition to staff permissions, managers can:

Delete an order (if verified to be incorrect)

Invite new users with the “Sale-Staff” role

Add, edit, delete products

Configure settings:

Conversation tags

Fast messages for chat

### C. Company Owner Workflow (Director role)

Company owners have full access including:

Staff and manager features

Promote/demote managers

Add/remove shops

Access advertisements management

View company operating costs

Access analytics:

Marketing costs

Salary payouts

Inventory statistics

Operational expenses

Access AI-powered features

#### Additional Permissions

Invite users into the company

Access multiple shop groups

#### Switching Views

When a user logs in, they will see:

Companies they created

Companies where they were invited as staff

Inside each company: a list of shop groups
